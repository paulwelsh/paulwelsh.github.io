Getting Started

Welcome to affiliates.esellerate.net. This is the place for managing all your activities as an eSellerate affiliate. You will use this site to:

  • Find products you would like to sell
  • Request approval from publishers to sell the products (when required)
  • Track your affiliate sales and activity through various reports

To get started selling as an eSellerate affiliate, follow these steps:

Sign up for your free membership

  1. If you have not yet signed up for your free membership, go to the home page of affiliates.esellerate.net and do one of the following:
    • Click the "Sign Up" link in the page header.
    • Click the "Sign Up For Free" button.
  2. Accept our license agreement.
  3. Enter your account information.
  4. Enter your payment information if it's available. Otherwise, you can enter it later.

Find products to sell

  1. Click Available Products.
  2. Use the search functions to find a product, or click a category link to browse the listings.
  3. Click the "Sell this product" link for a product that you'd like to sell.
  4. If necessary, follow the steps displayed to request approval to sell. You will receive e-mail notification when a publisher approves or declines your request to sell its products.

Make the products available to your affiliate sites and channels

If you are approved to sell the publisher's products, clicking the "Sell this product" link will take you to the Product Detail page. This page includes:

  • A Purchase Links tab, with information on how to link to the product in the publisher's web store
  • A Landing Pages tab (when available for the product), with information on how to link to a customized web page that promotes the product and typically provides both "Buy" and "Try" opportunities
  • An eST tab, with information on how to provide a trial version that will enable users to later purchase the product
