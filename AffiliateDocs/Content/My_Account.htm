My Account

This page contains information used to administer your account. Some fields are required.

As you enter your information, please note that the following fields will be available to publishers whose products you sell, much like you can view a publisher's contact information at the SKU Detail page of one of its products:

  • Affiliate ID
  • Affiliate Name
  • Web Site
  • Contact Name
  • Contact E-mail
  • Phone
  • Address, City, State, Postal Code, Country

Affiliate ID – This is your non-editable affiliate identifier.

Affiliate Name – Enter a name for your affiliate account.

First Name, Last Name – Enter the name of the primary contact for this account.

Address 1, Address 2, City, State, Postal Code, Country – Enter your address information.

Note: Your country of origin will determine the payment type set for you at My Payment Info.

Phone – Enter a phone number for the person listed as your primary contact.

Web Site – Enter the URL for your affiliate web site.

Contact E-mail – This will be the "From" address for e-mails sent for event notifications, such as when you request approval to sell a publisher's products. This address may also be used by publishers wanting more information about your available products. Enter only one e-mail address here.

Notification E-mail – This will be the "To" address where you will receive e-mails sent for event notifications, such as when a publisher approves your request to sell its products. It will also be the address where you will receive e-mail notification when eSellerate issues a payment for your sales. Enter only one e-mail address here.

Per Order E-mail – If you would like to receive e-mail notification of each order that credits a sale to you, enter the recipient address here. Enter only one e-mail address here.

Note: The Per Order E-mail does not include your profit or eSellerate's commission on your orders from 4/1/2011 and later. To find that information and more, please use Business Intelligence Reports.

Login Name – Enter the login name for your account, using at least eight characters but no more than 50. Case will not be considered. You can use any case for the login name when you log in to the system.

Password, Retype Password – Enter a password for your account, using at least eight characters but no more than 50. Case will be considered. You will need to use the correct case for the password when you log in to the system.

Affiliate Banner – For information on this option, click the provided "What's This" link.

Preferences – To receive e-mail listings of new products that become available to sell, check the corresponding box and select a frequency of daily, weekly or monthly.

Tax Payer ID Type, Tax Payer ID Number, Legal Name – In order for you to receive your payment, eSellerate must have a signed copy of Form W-9 or W-8BEN on file. Please see the onscreen text for more information on completing and sending the required form to eSellerate. You can then get your Tax Payer ID Type, Tax Payer ID Number and Legal Name from the completed form, and fill in that information here.

Note: If you are not ready to enter payment details but you need to save changes to some other part of the My Account page, you can select Other for Tax Payer ID Type and enter some placeholder text in the Legal Name field. This would be a temporary solution until you return to the page to enter payment details.

Note: The available choices for Tax Payer ID Type are SSN (social security number), EIN (employer identification number) , ITIN (individual taxpayer identification number) and Other. If none of the three predefined Tax Payer ID Types apply, select Other for Tax Payer ID Type, and enter your non-U.S. tax ID in the Tax Payer ID Number field.
