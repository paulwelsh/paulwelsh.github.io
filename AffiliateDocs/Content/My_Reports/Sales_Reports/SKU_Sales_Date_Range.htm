SKU Sales by Date Range

Note: The information that is available through this report does not include your profit or eSellerate's commission on your orders from 4/1/2011 and later. To find that information and more, please use Business Intelligence reporting.

Note: Beginning on 4/26/2011, we have temporarily enabled estimated costing information (your profit and our commission) for our on-screen reports. This short-term change will allow us to complete some enhancements to Business Intelligence and Business Navigator reports.

Use this report to see sales listed by SKU.

To run a SKU Sales by Date Range report:
  1. Use any of the popmenus (Publisher, SKU, Tracking ID and Date Range) to narrow the scope of your report, if desired. You can also edit or enter dates in the From and To fields.
  2. Click Run. This will display all SKU sales that matched your report criteria.
  3. For individual SKU sales information, click the appropriate SKU Name field.
