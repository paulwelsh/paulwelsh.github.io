My Payment Info

This page is for entering your payment information if it's available. You are not required to enter payment information to begin using the affiliate program. Until you enter this information, you will see the following reminder each time you log in to the system:

"You must complete the My Payment Information section before eSellerate can pay you for your sales. If this information is unavailable, you can enter it later."

Payment Methods

All payments will be in U.S. dollars. Depending on your country of origin as specified at the My Account page, eSellerate will send your payments by direct deposit via one of the following:

  • Automated Clearing House (ACH) – This is your payment type if you are located in the United States. The service is free of charge between U.S. banks.
  • International ACH – This is your payment type if you are located in one of the following countries:
    • Australia
    • Austria
    • Belgium
    • Canada
    • France
    • France, Metropolitan
    • French Guiana
    • French Polynesia
    • French Southern Territory
    • Germany
    • Italy
    • The Netherlands
    • The Netherlands Antilles
    • New Zealand
    • Spain
    • Switzerland
    • The United Kingdom

    There is no charge to you for these payments.

  • International wire transfer – This is your payment type if you are located in a country other than the ones listed above. There is a $15 fee per payment.

Your payment type will be noted above bank name on the My Payment Info page.

For detailed payment information, see the Compensation section of the Affiliate Agreement, which you can access anytime through a link on the My Account page.
