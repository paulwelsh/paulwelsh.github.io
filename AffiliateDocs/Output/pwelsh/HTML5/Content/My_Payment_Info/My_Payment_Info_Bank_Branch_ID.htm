Bank Branch Identification Number

If your payment type is international wire, your payment information will include a field for entering Routing/Branch ID. If you are required to provide this information, you should enter the bank branch identification number. This information is required if you are located in one of the following countries: Greece, Hong Kong, India, Ireland, Portugal, Russia or South Africa. If you are located in a country other than one of those listed, you can leave the Routing/Branch ID field blank.

Obtaining the Bank Branch Identification Number

Please ask your banker for your institution's bank branch identification number, which should match the format listed for your country below:

  • Greece – seven digits
  • Hong Kong – three digits
  • India – four alphabetical characters, followed by one digit, followed by six alphanumeric characters
  • Ireland – six digits
  • Portugal – eight digits
  • Russia – nine digits
  • South Africa – six digits
