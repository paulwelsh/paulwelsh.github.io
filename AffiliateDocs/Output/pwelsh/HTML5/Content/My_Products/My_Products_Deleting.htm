Deleting Products

To delete products from your account:
  1. Do one of the following to select the desired product(s):
    • To select individual products, check the box to the left of the product.
    • To select all products on the page, check the box in the table heading.
  2. Click Delete.
  3. When the confirmation dialog displays, click OK.
Notes:
  • You can only delete products on the currently displayed page.
  • If you have chosen to automatically sign up for all current and future products from a given publisher, deleting all of that publisher's products from your account will stop the process.
