Product Detail

Notes:
  • For access to information in the Purchase Links, Landing Pages and eST tabs of the Product Detail page, you need to be a member of eSellerate Affiliates and logged in. Help for accomplishing this will be provided when applicable.
  • There are two options for signing up to sell a publisher's products:
    • You can sign up for individual products.
    • You can sign up for all of the publisher's products. If you choose this option, all products that the publisher offers for affiliate sale will be added to your My Products listing, as will any products that the publisher offers in the future. You will receive e-mail notification for each automatic sign-up that occurs after the initial sign-up.

Finding Publisher Information

You can use the functions provided at the top portion of the Product Detail page to:

  • View the publisher's web site.
  • Send the publisher an e-mail.
  • View all products available from the publisher.

Selling the Product

The Product Detail page includes the following tabs:

  • Description – This is typically a longer description of the product than the one displayed in product listings.
  • Purchase Links – This provides instructions on how to sell the product through a "Buy" link on your affiliate web site.
  • Landing Pages – This provides instructions on how to sell the product through a link on your affiliate web site to a customized web page that promotes the product and typically provides both "Buy" and "Try" opportunities.
  • eST – This provides instructions on how to sell the product through a "Try" link on your affiliate web site, if a trial version is available.

If you want to sell the product displayed on the Product Detail page, note that you have two options:

  • You can sign up to sell the individual product.
  • You can sign up to sell all of the publisher's products.

Stopping Automatic Sign-up

If you no longer wish to automatically sign up for all future products from a given publisher, you can stop the process. Existing products will not be affected. To stop automatic sign-up, go to the Product Detail page of any one of the publisher's SKUs and click the "Stop automatic sign-up" link. When the confirmation dialog displays, click OK.
