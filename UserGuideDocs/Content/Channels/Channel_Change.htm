Channels: Change Control

When you accept a SKU that a publisher has submitted, copies the SKU and its accompanying product into your channel. Because the channel items are copies, you can customize individual settings if desired without affecting the source items within the publisher's account. However, the reverse is not always true for changes that the publisher can make to the source items, as explained here.

Some changes that the publisher can make to source SKUs will have an effect on the corresponding items in your channel. You will receive e-mail notification of these types of changes. One such notification is a notice of SKU deletion, which will instruct you to remove any links from your channel web site to the associated SKU as soon as possible. The other notifications fall into two categories: changes that you can accept or reject, and changes that are effective immediately for the associated SKU within your channel. If you have the option to accept or reject the publisher's changes, the notification will include instructions on how to do that. Those instructions also appear below.

To process SKU changes:
  1. Select a change that you want to review.
  2. To accept the change, which will update the associated SKU within your channel, click Accept. Otherwise, to reject the change, which will leave the associated SKU within your channel as is, click Reject.
  3. Repeat steps 1-2 for all pending SKU changes.

Note: There is no automated e-mail sent to the publisher when you accept or reject changes.
