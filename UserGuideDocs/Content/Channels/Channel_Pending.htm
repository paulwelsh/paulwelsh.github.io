Channels: Pending Channel SKUs

You will receive an e-mail notification each time a publisher submits SKUs for your channel to sell. The notification will include instructions on how to accept or decline the SKUs. Those instructions also appear below.

To process submitted SKUs:
  1. If the publisher's pending channel SKUs are not visible, use the search function to find them.
  2. Select a SKU that you want to process.
  3. Click the appropriate button to either accept or decline the SKU. automatically sends the publisher an e-mail notification of your decision.
  4. Repeat steps 2-3 to process any additional SKUs.
Notes on accepting SKUs

When you accept a SKU into your channel, automatically does the following:

  • Sends the publisher an e-mail notification of your acceptance (as previously noted)
  • Copies the publisher's SKU and its accompanying product into the channel
  • Adds the SKU to the Available Channel SKUs listing, with the default values of not in channel, not buy qualified and not try qualified
  • Adds the SKU to the Web Store eSeller that has the earliest creation date
  • Within the Web Store eSeller, sets the SKU's store category to No Category, and the SKU's status to Preview
  • Sets up the SKU's SplitPay relationships according to the terms of your channel agreement
