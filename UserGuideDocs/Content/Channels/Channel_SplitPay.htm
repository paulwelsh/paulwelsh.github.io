Channels: SplitPay Account Information

Before you can save changes to the Receipt & Payment Information page and submit SKUs to the channel, you need to enter your payment information. Your share of the proceeds from the channel's sales will go to the eSellerate SplitPay account that you specify here. If you do not have a SplitPay account, you can create one at http://SplitPay.eSellerate.net.

Note: As an publisher, you have the option to join the eSellerate SplitPay program, which enables publishers to split the sales proceeds from select SKUs with third parties. That program requires the third parties, or payees, to set up their own SplitPay accounts and provide you with the account IDs. In contrast, the Channels program requires you to set up a SplitPay account and provide the channel with the account ID, because in this instance you are the payee. (For more information on the SplitPay program, see Joining eSellerate SplitPay.)
