Creating SKUs

SKUs, or Stock Keeping Units, are the representations of what you sell to your customers.

Overview

Creating SKUs involves the following steps:

  • Creating the SKU and Setting Its Basic Properties
  • Setting the SKU's Advanced Properties (Optional)
  • Adding the SKU's Payees (Optional)
  • Setting Up the SKU as a Bundle (Optional)
  • Setting the SKU's Renewal Properties (Optional)
  • Adding the SKU to Web Store or Embedded Web Store eSellers
