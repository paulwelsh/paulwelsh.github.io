SKU Management

The SKU Management section of the Sales Manager is for completing the following tasks:

  • Creating SKUs
  • Setting Up Pricing
  • Creating eSellers
  • Creating Serial Numbers
  • Setting Up Product Activation
  • Importing External Serial Numbers for Activation
  • Manually Activating Serial Numbers

Note: If you're just getting started, you may find it helpful to create items in the following order. First, create any needed auxiliary items, such as serial numbers, downloads, volume discounts and terms of sale (in any order). Then, create SKUs and eSellers, respectively. When you follow this order, you'll have serial numbers available for your SKUs, SKUs for your eSellers, and so on.
