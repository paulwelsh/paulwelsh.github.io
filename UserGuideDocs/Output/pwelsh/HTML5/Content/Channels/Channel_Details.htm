Channel Details

Once you've partnered with a channel, the Channel Details page is the place for managing the relationship. From here you can:

  • Submit web store SKUs for the channel to sell
  • Assign categories to control where your SKUs appear within the channel
  • View a listing of the SKUs the channel sells for you
  • View the channel agreement and contact information
  • Edit your own contact information for use by the channel and its customers
  • Visit the channel by clicking the link at the top of the page
To submit SKUs for the channel to sell:
  1. For each SKU that you plan to submit, go to the Channel tab of the SKU Detail page and do the following:
    • Complete all applicable information under the Software Requirements and System Requirements sections. (Some attributes in those sections are required.)
    • If appropriate, specify a trial download in the Trialware section.
  2. From the Channel Details page, click Add SKUs.
  3. Select the SKUs and click Add Selected SKUs.
  4. Send the e-mail as instructed.

You will receive e-mail notification when the channel accepts or declines the submitted SKUs.

Next, you will need to assign at least one channel category to each SKU. These channel categories are separate from any other categories you may have assigned to the SKUs within the Sales Manager.

Note: Categories are the primary means of organization in the channel web site. Consequently, the choices you make for channel categories are important to the visibility of your offerings.

To assign channel categories to SKUs:
  1. From the Channel Details page, click the Categories link for the desired SKU. (If you have not yet assigned a channel category to the SKU, the link will read "Select Categories..." Otherwise, the link will display the name of the first selected category.) The Select SKU Channel Categories page appears.
  2. Follow the instructions provided to assign one or more channel categories to the SKU.
  3. Repeat this process for as many SKUs as needed.
