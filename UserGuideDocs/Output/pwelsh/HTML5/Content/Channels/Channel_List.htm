Channel List

The eSellerate Channels program enables sales partnerships between channel web sites and software publishers. If a channel offers to sell your products, you will work out an agreement with the channel offline, and then use the eSellerate Sales Manager to accept the agreement.

Notes on the eSellerate Channels program

  • To participate in the eSellerate Channels program, you need an eSellerate SplitPay account. If you do not have a SplitPay account, you can create one at http://SplitPay.eSellerate.net.
  • The Sales Manager provides three options for finding information about channel orders: the Orders search, the Returns search and the Channel SKU Sales by Date Range report. These are the only Sales Manager options that account for channel orders.
  • Customers that purchase your products through a channel are instructed to contact you for product support. To look up these customers' orders for support purposes, use the Orders search, which will list the orders under "Channel Orders" in the search results. The Orders search is available through Order Management.
  • The channel is responsible for product returns. Customers that purchase your products through a channel should contact that channel if they need to request refunds. To look up orders for returns, use the Returns search, which will specify when the channel should process the return. The Returns search is available through Order Management.
  • To monitor your channel sales activity, use the SKU Sales by Date Range report, which is available through Sales Reports. This report allows you to find channel sales information without having to log in to your SplitPay account.

How to accept a channel agreement

If a channel submits an agreement for you to accept, the Channel List page will display the channel name and a "Click to Accept Agreement" link. Click that link to go to the Channel Agreement page, where you can review and accept the agreement.

Next, you will need to complete the Receipt & Payment Information page. See Default Receipt Information and SplitPay Account Information.
