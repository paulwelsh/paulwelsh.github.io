Channel Order Detail

The Channel Order Detail page is accessible from the results of an Orders search. You can use the information on this page to provide product support for channel orders, as in the following examples:

  • This page shows the number of activations for a given serial number. If you need to manage the activations, you can click a link to go to the Activation Details page.
  • If a channel order includes one or more of your products that use the "publisher e-mailed" serial number option, you will receive an e-mail notification that specifies the order number. When you look up the order, you can use information such as registration name and customer e-mail address to provide the needed serial numbers.
  • To send an e-mail message to the customer, you can click the E-mail field.

Note: The channel is responsible for product returns. Customers that purchase your products through a channel should contact that channel if they need to request refunds.
