Embedded Web Store eSellers

With Embedded Web Store eSellers, you can add integrated purchase and update capabilities to your application, and control virtually every aspect of the store interface through the use of Custom Layouts.

When you turn your application into an Embedded Web Store eSeller, your customers can:

  • Purchase the retail version of your product directly from a demo version.
  • Use your retail software to check for newer versions, and upgrade directly from the application when a newer version becomes available. These new versions can be free or purchased upgrades.

Embedded Web Store eSellers can help you deliver the latest versions of your software at the latest price. Your customers always have instant access to your new releases.
