Running Sales Reports

The Sales Reports page provides access to the following reports:

  • SKU Sales by Date Range
  • SKU Sales by Currency
  • SKU Sales by Country
  • SKU Sales Summary
  • Subscription SKU Sales Summary
  • Transaction Ledger
  • Orders by Payment Type
  • Daily Summary File
  • SplitPay Transaction Export
  • Cross-Sell Activity by Primary SKU
  • Cross-Sell Activity by Group
  • Up-Sell Activity by Primary SKU
  • Up-Sell Activity by Group
  • Basic Affiliate Sales by Date Range
  • eSellerate Affiliate Sales by Date Range
