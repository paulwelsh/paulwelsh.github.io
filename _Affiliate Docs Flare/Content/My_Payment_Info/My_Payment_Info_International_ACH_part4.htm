International ACH Part 4

Help in locating the required information on checks from Spain, Switzerland and the United Kingdom follows.

Spain
The required information includes:

  • IBAN
  • Bank SWIFT BIC
  • A 9-digit international routing code for the bank, which consists of an eight-digit bank/branch code and a one-digit check digit
  • The currency of the country where the bank is located

Savings accounts can be used as long as they conform to the numbering described above. Savings accounts with internal bank account numbers that do not conform to the above standards cannot be used for eSellerate's international ACH option.

Switzerland
The required information includes:

  • IBAN
  • Bank SWIFT BIC
  • A three- to five-digit international routing code for the bank (called an SIC number)
  • The currency of the country where the bank is located

Savings accounts can be used as long as they conform to the numbering described above. Savings accounts with internal bank account numbers that do not conform to the above standards cannot be used for eSellerate's international ACH option.

The United Kingdom
The required information includes:

  • IBAN
  • Bank SWIFT BIC
  • A six-digit international routing code for the bank (called a sort code). If the sort code is less than six digits, add leading zeros.
  • The currency of the country where the bank is located

Building Society savings account cannot be used for eSellerate's international ACH option.
