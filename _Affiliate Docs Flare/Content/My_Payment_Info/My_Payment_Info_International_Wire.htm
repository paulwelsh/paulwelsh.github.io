International Wire

The My Payment Info page is for entering your payment information if it's available. You are not required to enter payment information to begin using the affiliate program. Until you enter this information, you will see the following reminder each time you log in to the system:

"You must complete the My Payment Information section before eSellerate can pay you for your sales. If this information is unavailable, you can enter it later."

Entering Payment Information for International Wire

The information to enter for payments by international wire transfer includes:

  • Bank account number
    • You will need to enter an IBAN if you are located in one of the following countries: Bulgaria, Cypress, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia or Sweden.
    • For countries other than the ones listed above, the information will be available on a check from the bank account you choose for receiving the wire transfers.
  • Bank SWIFT BIC
  • Bank Branch Identification Number
  • Intermediary Bank Information – An intermediary bank is a receiving bank other than the originator's bank or the beneficiary's bank. The information to enter for intermediary bank includes:
    • Bank name
    • Intermediary Bank ID
    • Bank SWIFT BIC
    • Intermediary bank address and phone information (optional)
    • Special instructions (optional)
