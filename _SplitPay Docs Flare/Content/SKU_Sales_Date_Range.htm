SKU Sales by Date Range

Note: The information that is available through this report does not include your profit or eSellerate's commission on your orders from 4/1/2011 and later. To find that information and more, please use Business Navigator reports.

Note: Beginning on 4/26/2011, we have temporarily enabled estimated costing information (your profit and our commission) for our on-screen reports. This short-term change will allow us to complete some enhancements to Business Navigator reports.

Use this report to see sales listed by SKU.

To run a SKU Sales by Date Range report:
  1. Use any of the popmenus (Publisher, SKU and Date Range) to narrow the scope of your report, if desired. You can also edit or enter dates in the From and To fields.
  2. Click Run. This will display all SKU sales that matched your report criteria.
  3. For individual SKU sales information, click the appropriate SKU Name field. This will display the SKU Sales Detail page.
  4. For individual order information, click the appropriate Order Number field. This will display the Order Detail page. The information available on this page includes:
    • Order details such as order ID, status, entry date and fulfillment date
    • (When access is granted by the publisher) customer details such as name, e-mail address, contact preference and serial numbers issued
    • Return information
