My Account

This page contains information used to administer your account. Some fields are required.

As you enter your information, please note that the following fields will be available to publishers:

  • SplitPay ID
  • Payee Name
  • Web Site
  • Contact E-mail
  • Phone
  • Address, City, State, Postal Code, Country

SplitPay ID – This is your non-editable payee identifier. You will need to send this ID to each eSellerate publisher that plans to add you to its list of payees.

Payee Name – Enter a name for your payee account.

First Name, Last Name – Enter the name of the primary contact for this account.

Address 1, Address 2, City, State, Postal Code, Country – Enter your address information.

Note: Your country of origin will determine the payment type set for you at My Payment Info.

Phone – Enter a phone number for the person listed as your primary contact.

Web Site – Enter the URL for your web site.

Contact E-mail – This address can be used by publishers to contact you about your SplitPay relationships. Enter only one e-mail address here.

Notification E-mail – This will be the "To" address where you will receive e-mail messages sent for event notifications, such as when a publisher adds your account to its list of payees. This field will accept multiple e-mail addresses separated by commas.

Per SKU E-mail – If you would like to receive e-mail notification of each SKU sale that credits a payment to you, enter the recipient address here. This field will accept multiple e-mail addresses separated by commas.

Note: The Per SKU E-mail does not include your profit or eSellerate's commission on your orders from 4/1/2011 and later. To find that information and more, please use Business Navigator Reports.

Login Name – Enter the login name for your account, using at least eight characters but no more than 50. Case will not be considered. You can use any case for the login name when you log in to the system.

Password, Retype Password – Enter a password for your account, using at least eight characters but no more than 50. Case will be considered. You will need to use the correct case for the password when you log in to the system.

Tax Payer ID Number, Legal Name – In order for you to receive your payment, eSellerate must have a signed copy of Form W-9 or W-8BEN on file. Please see the onscreen text for more information on completing and sending the required form to eSellerate. You can then get your Tax Payer ID Number and Legal Name from the completed form, and fill in that information here. 

Note: If you are not ready to enter payment details but you need to save changes to some other part of the My Account page, you can select (select one) for Tax Payer ID Number, and leave the Legal Name field blank.
