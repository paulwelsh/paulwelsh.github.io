Channels: Default Receipt Information

Use this dialog to enter two types of information:

  • Your product support information for use by the channel's customers
  • Your contact information for use by the channel

Required fields are marked with an asterisk.

Entering Your Product Support Information

Text that you enter in the Company Name and Receipt E-mail fields will accompany your product's information in receipts generated for channel sales. The message will read as follows:

If you require assistance with this product, please contact <Company Name> at <Receipt E-mail>. They will be able to help you with the following:
     * Installation questions
     * Software support
     * Upgrades
     * General questions specific to this product

Entering Your Contact Information

Text that you enter in the following fields will define your contact information for use by the channel: Company Name, Address 1, Address 2, City, State, Postal Code, Country, Channel Contact E-mail, URL and Phone.

Note: In addition to providing the channel with a way to contact you by e-mail, the Channel Contact E-mail field defines the "From" address for e-mails sent for event notifications, such as when you submit a SKU to the channel. This field also defines the "To" address where you will receive e-mails sent for event notifications, such as when a channel accepts a SKU that you submitted.
