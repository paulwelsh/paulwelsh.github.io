Channel Publishers

The eSellerate Channels program enables sales partnerships between channel web sites and software publishers. If eSellerate invites you to join the program as a channel, you will work out an agreement with eSellerate offline, and then use the eSellerate Sales Manager to manage your channel offerings.

To add an eSellerate publisher to your channel:
  1. Contact a publisher whose products you would like to sell.
  2. Work out an agreement that allows your channel web site to sell the publisher's products.
  3. Submit the agreement to your eSellerate contact, who will make it available in the Sales Manager for the publisher to review and accept. When the publisher accepts the agreement, the publisher's information will appear in your Channel Publishers list.

Next, the publisher can submit SKUs for your channel to sell.
