Uploading eCD Files

When you have your files ready for use with eCD, you can upload them as described here.

To upload eCD files:
  1. Click the Downloads tab. The Downloads page appears.
  2. Click New. The Download Detail page appears.
  3. Follow the instructions provided in the help for that page. You will have the option of designating the upload for eCD use only.
