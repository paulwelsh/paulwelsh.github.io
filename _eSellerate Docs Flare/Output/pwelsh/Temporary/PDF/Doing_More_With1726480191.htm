Doing More with eSellerate

From here you can start implementing features to increase the sales of your products through eSellerate, and to protect the digital goods that you sell.

Some of the options that are available to you include:

  • Cross-selling – Promote one or more SKUs in addition to the ones your customers select for their purchases. See "Creating Cross-Sells" on page 1.
  • Up-selling – Promote higher priced alternatives to the SKUs your customers select for their purchases. See "Creating Up-Sells" on page 1.
  • Bundles – Sell a collection of SKUs as a bundle. See "Setting Up the SKU as a Bundle (Optional)" on page 1.
  • Coupons – Use coupon discounts to drive customer traffic to your store and increase sales. See "Creating Coupon Discounts" on page 1.
  • eCD – When customers purchase software from your web store, give them the option to also purchase backup copies on CD. Or sell CDs as items that are always included with certain SKUs. See "Configuring eCD" on page 1.
  • eDP – You earn $1 each time your customers purchase eSellerate Download Protection (eDP). This service guarantees customers the ability to re-download exact copies of the files they purchased, for one year from the date of purchase. See "Presenting the eSellerate Download Protection (eDP) Option to Customers" on page 1.
  • Subscriptions – Provide your customers with alternatives to paying a lump sum for products and services. See "Creating Subscription Methods" on page 1.
  • Embedded Web Store eSellers – Add integrated purchase and update capabilities to your application, and control virtually every aspect of the store interface through the use of Custom Layouts. See "Embedded Web Store eSellers" on page 1.
  • eSellerate Product Activation – Protect your software against casual software piracy. See "Creating Product Activation Sets" on page 1.
  • Localization – Use localized stores to sell to your customers in their preferred languages. See "Localizing eSellerate Stores" on page 1.
  • eSellerate Affiliates – Take advantage of our full-featured affiliates program and let others sell your products for you. See "eSellerate Affiliates" on page 1.
